1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember your preferences, and provide analytical insights to site owners.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Our cookie banner: When you first visit our site, you can accept or reject non-essential cookies.
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Opt-out links: Google Analytics opt-out

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
